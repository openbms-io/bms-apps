Theme 5: Dark Energy

This theme combines the best of both worlds: tastytrade's dark, minimal, professional aesthetic with vibrant gradient accents that make work feel energizing, not tedious. The pure black background provides maximum focus, while the purple-pink gradient adds modern energy to critical actions. Minimal visual noise, maximum impact.

Why this works for BMS: The dark UI reduces eye strain during long configuration sessions, the minimal style keeps focus on the work (not the UI), and the vibrant accents make the 223P popup feel like critical, valuable work.

Alternatives: Theme 2 (Neon Gradient) for lighter background, Theme 3 (Cyber Green) for futuristic boldness.
